Welcome

This website allows you to view/browse live duel statistics in Yu-Gi-Oh! Master Duel. We do not sell you publicly accessible data here so enjoy this useful service entirely for free.

Statistics

In the following you will see various live duel statistics. Our data is 100% genuinely collected from the game's matchmaking history and not faked, manually uploaded or discriminated against. We do not fabricate fake tier lists or setup rankings based on very small sample sizes like MasterDuelMeta who only have a few 100 duels available (see the issues with small sample sizes).

Frequently Asked Questions

How to contribute data?

Download and run JMaster Duel Bot, then simply play in Ranked to contribute your matchmaking statistics automatically.

Note: If you want to opt-out (not contribute any matchmaking statistics), you can do that by disabling the option "Send Matchmaking Statistics" in the Settings tab.
